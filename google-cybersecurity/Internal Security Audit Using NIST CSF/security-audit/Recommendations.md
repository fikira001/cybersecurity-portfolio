## Recommendations

Based on the security audit, Botium Toys should prioritize strengthening its security posture by implementing both technical and administrative controls.

### Priority Recommendations

- **Implement the Principle of Least Privilege (PoLP)** to ensure employees only have access to the systems and data required for their roles.
- **Develop and maintain a disaster recovery plan** to support business continuity and minimize downtime during security incidents.
- **Strengthen password policies** by enforcing strong password requirements, multi-factor authentication (MFA), and regular password updates.
- **Implement separation of duties** to reduce the risk of fraud and unauthorized access to critical business functions.
- **Deploy an Intrusion Detection System (IDS)** to improve visibility into malicious or suspicious network activity.
- **Establish a regular backup strategy** for critical systems and data, including periodic testing of backup restoration procedures.
- **Encrypt sensitive data** both at rest and in transit to protect customer and business information from unauthorized disclosure.
- **Implement a password management solution** to securely store and manage organizational credentials.
- **Formalize maintenance procedures for legacy systems** to reduce vulnerabilities associated with outdated infrastructure.

### Compliance Recommendations

To improve compliance with industry standards and regulations, Botium Toys should:

- Restrict access to customer payment information using role-based access controls.
- Implement encryption to protect payment card data and personally identifiable information (PII).
- Classify and inventory organizational assets based on sensitivity and business importance.
- Establish documented security and privacy policies that align with PCI DSS, GDPR, and SOC best practices.
- Conduct periodic security audits and risk assessments to ensure ongoing compliance and continuous improvement.

## Summary

The audit identified several gaps in access control, data protection, incident preparedness, and compliance. Addressing these findings will significantly improve Botium Toys' ability to protect sensitive information, reduce operational risk, and meet industry security standards.
